aCart Seller

The Flutter online seller app is a user-friendly and customizable mobile application that empowers individuals and businesses to create their own digital storefronts, reach a broader audience, and manage their online sales seamlessly. Whether you're a small business owner, a budding entrepreneur, or an established e-commerce brand, this app provides the tools you need to showcase and sell your products or services efficiently.

1.User Registration and Profiles: Users can create accounts with their personal information.User profiles display contact details, order history, and saved preferences.

2.Product Listing and Management: Sellers can add, edit, and remove product listings.Include product details such as title, description, price, and images.Organize products into categories or collections for easy browsing.

3.Search and Filter Options:Robust search functionality with filters for users to find products quickly.Filters by price range, category, ratings, and more

4.Shopping Cart: Users can add products to their shopping carts for later purchase.Ability to view and edit cart contents before checkout.

5.Secure Payment Processing: Integration with secure payment gateways for online transactions.Support for multiple payment methods such as credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and more

6.Order Tracking:Real-time order tracking with status updates.Notifications for order confirmation, shipping, and delivery..

7.Reviews and Ratings:Users can leave reviews and ratings for products.Build trust and credibility with customer feedback.

8.Messaging and Customer Support:In-app messaging for buyers and sellers to communicate.Access to customer support for inquiries and issue resolution.

9.Push Notifications:Notify users about promotions, discounts, and order updates via push notifications.

10.Inventory Management:Inventory tracking to prevent overselling.Low stock alerts for sellers.

11.Analytics and Reporting:Sellers can access sales analytics and reports.Insights on top-selling products, revenue trends, and customer behavior.

12.Customization:Personalize your store's branding and design.Customize product listings with rich media content.

13.Security and Privacy:Implement robust security measures to protect user data and transactions.

14.Multi-Language and Currency Support:Support for multiple languages and currencies to cater to a global audience.

15.Integration with Social Media:Share products on social media platforms to increase visibility.

16.Feedback and Ratings for Sellers:Buyers can rate and leave feedback for sellers, creating trust within the community.

17.Admin Panel:An admin panel to manage users, monitor transactions, and moderate content.

18.Shipping and Delivery Options:Integration with shipping providers for real-time shipping calculations.Offer various delivery options to customers.

19.Promotions and Discounts:Create and manage promotional campaigns and discount codes.

20.Cross-Platform Compatibility:The app is built using Flutter, ensuring compatibility with both Android and iOS devices.
