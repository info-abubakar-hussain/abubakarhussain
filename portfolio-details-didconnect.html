D-iDconnect — Your All-in-One Solution for Secure Digital Identity Management

Introducing D-iDconnect — your all-in-one solution for secure, personalized, and seamless D-iD (Decentralized Identity) management.

Safeguard your online presence and take full control of your digital identity (D-iD) with a powerful set of features designed for security, privacy, and convenience. D-iDconnect revolutionizes the way you manage your digital identity, giving you the power to create, manage, and protect your digital profile with ease. With advanced features like biometric authentication, consent-based interactions, and secure single sign-on, D-iDconnect ensures your online experience is both secure and seamless.

Whether you're managing personal profiles, connecting with business networks, or simplifying online access — D-iDconnect empowers you to stay in control of your DID identity across platforms. Built on blockchain technology and powered by AI, D-iDconnect offers a comprehensive solution for decentralized identity management.

Key Features

1. Secure Profile Management

Effortlessly craft and manage your DID digital profile. Control your personal details, biometrics, and preferences — all with maximum security and privacy. Your data, your way.

2. BioPass Biometric Authentication

Advanced security features including facial recognition and BioPass fingerprint biometric authentication for secure access to your digital identity. Integrated BioPass ID Fingerprint SDK frontend SDK with Flutter and connected to .NET Core backend models for seamless biometric verification. Multi-factor authentication combining biometrics, mnemonics, and secret phrases ensures maximum protection with enterprise-grade security.

3. Face Recognition (InsightFace)

Advanced facial recognition system powered by InsightFace — an open-source 2D/3D face analysis project. Implemented with SCRFD-10GF detection model for high-accuracy face detection and ResNet50 recognition model for identity verification. Features include liveness detection, anti-spoofing mechanisms, and 3D face mapping for secure biometric authentication.

4. OCR Document Verification (Qwen2)

Intelligent OCR-based document verification system powered by Qwen2 AI model (Qwen2-7B-Instruct). Automated identity document scanning, data extraction, and fraud detection for secure user onboarding. Supports multiple document types including national IDs, passports, and driver's licenses with high accuracy text recognition.

5. Wallet Creation & Management

Create and manage cryptocurrency wallets with secure mnemonic phrase generation. Store and manage your digital assets with enterprise-grade security.

6. User Profile Management

Complete user profile creation and management system. Update and modify your profile information with ease while maintaining data integrity and privacy.

7. Business Profile System

Create and manage business profiles with comprehensive company information. Establish your business presence in the decentralized identity ecosystem.

8. Business to Individual Connections

Build and manage trusted connections between businesses and individuals. Enable secure communication and collaboration across professional networks.

9. Business to Business Connections

Facilitate secure B2B networking and collaboration. Connect with other businesses, share credentials, and build professional partnerships with complete privacy and security.

10. Real-Time XMPP Chat

Communicate securely with friends, colleagues, and businesses using D-iDconnect's encrypted messaging platform powered by XMPP and Ejabberd. Enjoy private and reliable conversations without compromise. Send, receive, and save images, videos, and documents directly to your device's storage (gallery or file manager) for future access — keeping important files at your fingertips anytime.

11. Consent-Based Interaction

Experience true privacy with consent-based interactions. You control when and how your data is shared — building trust in every online experience.

12. Single Sign-On (SSO)

Simplify your digital life with single sign-on. Log in to multiple platforms using your D-ID identity, with one secure set of credentials — no more managing multiple passwords.

13. Advanced Search with Multiple Filters

Powerful search functionality with filtering options across multiple professions and industries. Find and connect with the right people or businesses quickly and efficiently.

14. Complete Admin Panel

Comprehensive admin dashboard to manage users, businesses, and connections. Monitor system activity, manage permissions, and ensure platform security. Complete control over user accounts, business profiles, and network connections.

15. Blockchain Integration

Built on blockchain technology to ensure decentralized, tamper-proof identity management. Your identity data is securely stored and managed on the blockchain.

16. AI-Powered Features

Leveraging artificial intelligence for facial recognition, voice authentication, and intelligent profile matching. Enhanced security and user experience through AI-driven solutions.

17. Mnemonic Phrase System

Secure wallet and account recovery using mnemonic phrases. Store and manage your recovery phrases with maximum security to ensure you never lose access to your digital identity.
