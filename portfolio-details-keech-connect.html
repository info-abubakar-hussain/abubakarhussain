Keech Connect - Social Networking Platform

Keech Connect is a modern social networking platform designed to connect people and build meaningful relationships. Built with Flutter for cross-platform compatibility, this application provides a seamless social experience for users in Canada and beyond.

The platform enables users to create profiles, connect with friends, share content, and engage with their social network through various interactive features. With a focus on user experience and modern design, Keech Connect brings people together in a secure and engaging environment.

Key Features

1. User Profiles

Create and customize personal profiles with photos, bio, and personal information. Manage privacy settings and control who can view your profile.

2. Social Connections

Connect with friends, family, and colleagues. Send and receive friend requests with an intuitive connection system.

3. Content Sharing

Share photos, videos, and status updates with your network. Express yourself through multimedia content sharing.

4. Real-Time Messaging

Private messaging system for one-on-one conversations. Group chat functionality for connecting with multiple friends simultaneously.

5. News Feed

Personalized news feed showing updates from your connections. Like, comment, and share posts to engage with your network.

6. Notifications

Real-time push notifications for friend requests, messages, and interactions. Stay connected and never miss important updates.

7. Search and Discovery

Search for users, posts, and content across the platform. Discover new connections and trending content.

8. Privacy Controls

Comprehensive privacy settings to control who can see your content and contact you. Block and report features for user safety.

9. Cross-Platform Support

Available on both Android and iOS platforms. Seamless experience across devices with synchronized data.

10. Media Management

Upload and manage photos and videos. Gallery view for easy access to shared media content.
