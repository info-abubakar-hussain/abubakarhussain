eezly - Your Ultimate Food Companion

FoodieFinder is a convenient and feature-rich mobile app that allows you to explore a wide range of restaurants, browse menus, place food orders, and compare prices from multiple restaurants in your area. Whether you're craving pizza, sushi, burgers, or any other cuisine, FoodieFinder helps you satisfy your appetite while saving money.

1.Restaurant Search and Discovery: Search for restaurants based on location, cuisine, or specific dishes.Discover new and popular dining spots in your area.Read restaurant reviews and ratings from other users.

2.Menu Exploration: Browse detailed menus with descriptions, prices, and images.View dietary information and filter options for dietary preferences.Mark your favorite dishes for quick access in the future.

3.Food Ordering:Easily add items to your cart.Customize your order by specifying preferences (e.g., spice level, toppings).Choose delivery or pickup options.Apply discounts and promo codes for savings.

4.Price Comparison:Compare prices for the same dish from multiple restaurants.Find the best deals and promotions.Save money by selecting the most affordable option.

5.User Profiles: Create a personal profile with your information and payment details.Save multiple delivery addresses for convenience.Keep track of your order history.

6.Real-time Tracking:Track the status of your order in real-time.Receive notifications when your food is being prepared, on its way, or delivered.

7.Reviews and Ratings:Leave reviews and ratings for restaurants and dishes.Read reviews to make informed choices.Contribute to the community by sharing your food experiences.

8.In-App Chat Support:Contact customer support for any issues or inquiries.Get assistance with order-related problems.

9.Notifications and Alerts:Receive notifications for order confirmations, promotions, and restaurant updates.Set preferences for notifications based on your interests.

10.Secure Payments:Make secure payments using various methods (credit card, mobile wallet, cash on delivery).Store payment information for quick and easy checkout.

11.Favorite and Repeat Orders:Save your favorite restaurants and dishes for quick reordering.Access a history of your previous orders for easy reference.

12.Restaurant Profiles:Explore detailed restaurant profiles with photos, operating hours, and contact information.See restaurant ratings and reviews from other users.

13.Location-based Services:Utilize GPS to find restaurants nearby.Get accurate estimated delivery times based on your location.

14.Multi-language and Multi-currency Support:Switch between different languages and currencies for a user-friendly experience.

15.Social Sharing:Share your food orders and dining experiences on social media.

16.Cross-Platform Compatibility:The app is built using Flutter, ensuring compatibility with both Android and iOS devices.
